The College Students Guide to Travelling in London

An overview of London

If you are reading this you are probably heading to London for the first time ever without your parents. This might even be your first time travelling by yourself! Yes, this might sound scary and your mind is racing all over the place trying to figure out where to stay, where to eat, what to do, etc. Well, you came to the right site. Here I will tell you my own experinces on travelling in London for the first time and give you some useful tips on how to maneuver this wonderful city! Hopefully at the end of reading this site you have a solid plan on what to do in London and this becomes one of the best trips of your life!
